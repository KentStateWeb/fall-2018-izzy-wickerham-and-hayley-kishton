The Fridge



Fresh Ideas // Chill People

The Fridge is a student-led major-neutral co-working space designed to encourage students to collaborate broadly on projects, business, and their goals. We apply Design Thinking strategies toward thought and process, and infuse the diverse skills of our attendees and leaders toward our general mission.
